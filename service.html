FAQ

How long will a project take to complete?

This is dependent on the project's size and specifications, including the quantity of painted rooms or floors, the amount of prep work required, and the need for touch-ups. Additionally, the weather needs to be taken into account.

Who will paint my house?

Our devoted staff of painters is skilled and knowledgeable about our paints in-depth. They are skilled in the methods for applying products to produce effects that are professional.

Is there any supervisor during the procedure?

Despite the fact that our painters have extensive training, we closely monitor your project using technology and one-on-one care to ensure a smooth delivery.

Is my package insured?

May Decor offers a satisfaction guarantee, and if you're not happy with your purchase, we'll refund your money or send you a replacement. Please get in touch with our customer support team via LIVE CHAT or email if your delivery arrives misplaced or damaged.

Is the picture on your website a true representation of what I will receive?

We aim for extremely high image accuracy. The visual depiction, however, could not always be accurate or the prints might differ. You have 45 days to return a goods for a refund if you are dissatisfied with it.

Can I ship to a P.O. Box?

Yes, we can deliver items that are less than 11 x 14 to a P.O. box. Larger products need to be delivered to a mailing address.
