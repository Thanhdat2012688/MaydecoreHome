About

Who we ara

Bring Arts to Your Life

In Hawaii, May Decor is the top provider of framed and stretched print-on-demand canvas wall art. We have made high-quality art prints available from internationally renowned and up-and-coming artists.

Our top aims are to offer the most wide selection of genres to enliven and remodel rooms while also offering the greatest quality art reproduction at an accessible price. To inspire a life filled with authenticity through timeless wall art that is available to everyone, which in turn supports and empowers artists, is our purpose and what drives everything we do.

Our Mission

Decorate your room with our paintings

Our aim at May Decor is to provide you with a huge selection of high-quality, reasonably priced art for your home. It's that easy. We recognize that choosing art is a very personal decision. Your wall decor reveals something special about you. We want to assist you in creating a stunning reflection of who you are in your house because, let's face it, you deserve a masterpiece.

Features

Why choose our paintings?

We take pride in offering the best price on canvas prints. Simply get in touch with our support staff and we'll match any lower price you find for the same size and print.

We have streamlined the art-buying experience with our curated collections of current trends and room settings, as well as a variety of genres, because we recognize that art selection is a process and that it may take time to locate canvas wall art that represent your style and your real self. With original and genuine wall art that may be updated annually or enjoyed forever, our goal is to enliven and inspire our clients.

With our native printing technique, our canvas prints are printed to order, guaranteeing that you receive the size you want. We provide them to you as soon as we can, keeping you informed at every stage. Please let us know if you're not entirely satisfied with your order! We'll fix it right now.

Free shipping is available on all orders placed within the United States!
